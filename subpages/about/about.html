Our Mission

Centralizing campus information under one platform to facilitate the development of students' civic identity and promote social interaction


The Problem

Students want to go to events but:

1. Every organization uses different outlets
2. It is hard to keep up
3. Students don't go to events



Our Solution

A collaborative calendar that event planners can add to spontaneously and without approval processes or overhead
